Privacy Policy for Chess Pi

Last Updated: October 27, 2023

Welcome to Chess Pi! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our chess application. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data.

Note: Chess Pi is primarily a client-side application that runs in your browser. Most data is stored locally on your device, and we minimize data collection to provide you with the best experience while respecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

Chess Pi does not require account creation or personal information to play the game. The only information you might provide includes:

  • Game preferences (theme, language, difficulty level) stored locally on your device
  • PGN files you choose to import for game analysis
  • Payment information if you choose to use the Pi Network payment feature (processed through Pi Network)

b) Automatically Collected Information

Our application may collect certain information automatically:

  • Game statistics and performance data (stored locally)
  • Device information (browser type, screen resolution) for optimization purposes
  • Cookies or local storage to remember your preferences

c) Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • chess.js library for game logic
  • i18next for internationalization
  • Font Awesome for icons
  • Pi Network for payment processing (if used)

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the chess game functionality
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To process payments (if you use the Pi Network payment feature)
  • To improve game performance and user experience
  • To analyze game patterns and difficulty balancing (anonymous, aggregated data only)

3. Data Storage and Local Processing

Chess Pi is designed to work primarily on your local device:

  • Game progress and settings are stored in your browser's local storage
  • PGN files you import are processed locally and not transmitted to our servers
  • All game analysis happens on your device without sending data to external servers

Important: As a client-side application, your game data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

4. Payment Processing

If you choose to use the refill feature with Pi Network payment:

  • Payment processing is handled by Pi Network
  • We do not store your payment information on our systems
  • Transaction details are subject to Pi Network's privacy policy
  • We only receive confirmation of successful payments to enable feature refills

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Client-side data encryption where applicable
  • Secure communication protocols for any data transmission
  • Regular security assessments of our code and infrastructure

However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal data from our systems
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your information
  • Data portability (receiving your data in a structured format)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

You can clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser's cache and local storage for our application.

8. Children's Privacy

Chess Pi is suitable for all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
